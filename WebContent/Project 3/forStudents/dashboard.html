University of Maryland 2011 EARS Database

Help

Search this year's reported accidents or observe patterns. Use the sliders or drop-down boxes in the Control Panel to adjust the range of months and range of time (by 24-hour clock). Enter a word or phrase to filter accidents. The table, chart, and map automatically update to match the range and/or filter in the Control Panel. Click on the column headers in the table to sort by that column in ascending or descending order. Click on the flags in the map to get information about the accident.
